Adoption Monitor: AI Enabled Workflows

People use AI tools and capabilities in support of activities, workflows, and use cases. What is often missing is the ability to connect all of these things together and pair them with utilization data, so teams can better understand the extent to which AI is being adopted and used on a sustained and growing basis. Imagine if anyone could define their own, their team's, or their organization's workflows, activities, and use cases; map supporting AI capabilities; and monitor AI capability adoption on an ongoing, automatic basis. This tool illustrates what that might look like.
